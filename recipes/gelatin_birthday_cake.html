Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of gelatin powder (any flavor you like)
  • 2 cups of boiling water
  • 1 cup of cold water
  • 1 can of whipped cream
  • 1 packet of candles
  • 1 large cake pan
    •

    Instructions:

    Dissolve the gelatin powder in the boiling water and stir well. Add the cold water and stir again. Pour the mixture into the cake pan and refrigerate until firm (about 4 hours). Remove the cake from the pan and place it on a large plate. Decorate the cake with whipped cream and candles. Sing ‚ÄúHappy Birthday‚Äù and blow out the candles. Cut the cake into slices and enjoy.

    Time:

    4 hours and 15 minutes

    Tip:

    If you want to make the cake more festive, you can add some fruit, nuts, or candy to the gelatin mixture before refrigerating it. Just make sure they are soft enough for your gums

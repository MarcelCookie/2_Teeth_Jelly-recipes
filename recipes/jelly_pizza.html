Ingredients:

  • 3 packets of gelatin powder (different flavors for different layers)
  • 6 cups of boiling water
  • Cooking spray or oil
  • A round cake pan

Instructions:

Choose one flavor of gelatin powder and dissolve it in 2 cups of boiling water. Stir well and pour into the cake pan. Refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours. Repeat with the other two flavors of gelatin powder, making sure to let each layer set before adding the next one. You should end up with a three-layered jelly cake. Carefully remove the jelly cake from the pan and place it on a large plate. Cut into slices and serve as pizza. Enjoy your jelly pizza with a spoon or a straw!

Time:

6 hours

Tip:

You can make your jelly pizza more colorful by using different flavors of gelatin powder. For example, you can use strawberry, lemon and lime for a red-yellow-green pizza. Or you can use grape, orange and blueberry for a purple-orange-blue pizza. The possibilities are endless!
